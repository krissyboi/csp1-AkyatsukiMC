Buruwisan Falls
Buruwisan Falls

Cold breeze of Buruwisan Falls, Head on over to the eastern part of Laguna and be amazed at the number of wonders found at the foot of Mt. Romelo. The townsmen of Siniloan say that the mountain flaunts more than 9 waterfalls, all of which are a perfect retreat for this tropical heat we're all experiencing.
